Welcome to the 2019 LATAM Legal Hackers Summit!

Since its founding in 2012, the Legal Hackers movement has grown into the largest grassroots legal innovation community in the world.

São Paulo Legal Hackers are hosting the first edition of the LATAM Legal Hackers Summit, that will convene chapter organizers and legal innovation thought leaders to share experiences and knowledge and to chart the path ahead for the Legal Hackers movement.

Our team is thrilled to be hosting the Summit. Can't wait to see you there!
What
Full open day summit conference with workshops, keynote speakers, panel discussions and huge networking; and an invitation-only organizers retreat.
When
April 12, 13 and 14

Important notes
Attire
Summit attire is smart casual/jeans etc.
Hotels and Hostels
We recommend staying near downtown (around Paulista Avenue) or near the event (around Faria Lima Avenue/Vila Olimpia). Check some options here
Weather
Sao Paulo in April is hot and humid (hottest days and cooler nights). Check the weather here.
Getting around
Taxis, Ubers, 99Taxis, Cabify etc. are plentiful, but the subway/bus is always cheaper (R$ 4,30). Check the status of the subway operation here.
Voltage and Sockets
In Sao Paulo the voltage is 110 V and the power plugs and sockets are of type "N". We recommend that you bring a universal plug adapter.
Wi-fi/SIMs
The Summit will have wi-fi. Let us know if you need options for SIMs.
